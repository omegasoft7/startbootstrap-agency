Carpooling solution

White label and open source solution

A white label and open source carpooling solution for Businesses or Individuals. Users can configure, customize and publish their Apps(Android and iOS) within a few minutes. It is fully customizable and can be used for free by hosting it in user servers. CAAS is the first open source carpooling solution. The product is result of having an experience and motivated team with years of experience in mobility and software development.

Event Image Processor(EIP)

White label solution for event photographers

First white label solution for image processing in an event. Imagine that you are a photographer in a huge event. You will take many pictures from different people in different situation. How do you find who was in each picture? How do you deliver pictures to the participants? How do you even monetize? We have a solution for all of these in just one package. with EIP you can take pictures and connect those pictures to bracelets or tickets of participants(A smart process in EIP will do it for you in background). After the event you make your final tweaks and will pass the pictures to the participants of the event. Participants will check the images and will make order to buy the pictures or even print it in a product (for instance wall decore or cup). You just click finish and EIP will handle the rest. We will sell images and sell products and will deliver them to user instead of you. You can also change the price and availibility of each product according to the type of your event to get the most revenue out of it.
